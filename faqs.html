FAQs

Your questions, answered

Do you offer free quotes?

Yes — every quote is free and with no obligation. We’ll inspect the roof, explain what needs doing in plain English and give you a clear written price with no pressure to go ahead.

Which areas do you cover?

We cover Nottingham, Derby & Mansfield, Arnold, Ilkeston, Belper and the surrounding East Midlands. If you’re nearby and not sure, just give us a call.

Are you local?

Yes. Coburn Roofing is a local, owner-run team based around Nottingham. When you call, you deal with the people actually doing the work — not a call centre.

Do you do emergency repairs?

Yes. If water’s coming in, call 07896 410119 and we’ll do our best to get to you quickly, make the roof safe and prevent further damage.

How do I get a quote?

Call or WhatsApp us on 07896 410119, or send a message through the contact form. We’ll arrange a convenient time to take a look and get your quote over to you.

Do you clean up after the work?

Always. We treat your property with respect, protect the areas we work around and leave the site clean and tidy when we’re finished.

How much does a new roof or repair cost?

Every roof is different, so we don’t do one-size-fits-all pricing. We give free written quotes based on the actual job, the materials you choose and access — so you know exactly what you’re paying before anything starts.

Do you use scaffolding?

For larger jobs and re-roofs, yes — it’s safer and gives a better finish, and we arrange it as part of the job. Smaller repairs may only need ladders or a tower.

Do you guarantee your work?

We stand behind our workmanship and only use quality materials. We’ll talk you through what’s covered for your specific job when we quote.

How soon can you start?

It depends on our current schedule and the type of work, but we’ll always give you a realistic timescale. For emergency leaks we prioritise getting you made safe quickly.

Still got a question?

Give us a call or a message — we’re happy to help and quotes are always free.
