Roof repairs with Coburn Roofing

Coburn Roofing carries out all types of roof repair across Nottingham, Derby and Mansfield — from a single slipped tile to leaks, storm damage and ridge re-bedding. We find the actual cause of the problem (not just the symptom), give you an honest quote, and can often attend quickly for urgent leaks.

A small roof problem rarely stays small. A slipped tile or cracked flashing lets water in, and by the time you see a damp patch on the ceiling the timbers and insulation are often already affected. Catching it early is almost always cheaper.

We repair pitched and flat roofs of every type — replacing broken or slipped tiles and slates, re-bedding ridge and hip tiles, renewing lead flashings, clearing and sealing valleys, and tracing stubborn leaks back to their source. Whatever the issue, you’ll get a straight answer and a fair price.

What’s included

  • Replacing slipped, cracked or missing tiles & slates
  • Re-bedding and re-pointing ridge and hip tiles
  • New lead flashings around chimneys and abutments
  • Leak detection and tracing to the true source
  • Valley repairs and re-lining
  • Emergency make-safe and temporary weatherproofing

Common roof problems we fix

  • Water stains or damp on upstairs ceilings and walls
  • Tiles or slates on the ground after high winds
  • Daylight visible in the loft
  • Loose, cracked or missing ridge tiles
  • Damp around the chimney or where the roof meets a wall

FAQs

Roof repairs — your questions answered

Do you do emergency roof repairs?

Yes. If you’ve got water coming in, call us on 07896 410119 and we’ll do our best to attend quickly, make the roof safe and stop further damage before arranging a permanent repair.

Can you find a leak if I don’t know where it’s coming from?

Yes. Leaks often travel along timbers before they show inside, so the damp patch is rarely directly under the fault. We trace leaks back to the real source so the repair actually lasts.

Is it worth repairing or should I replace the roof?

If the roof is generally sound and the problem is localised, a repair is the sensible option. If it’s failing in several places, we’ll tell you honestly and let you weigh up a re-roof — the choice is always yours.

Will you look at the roof for free?

Yes — we provide free inspections and written quotes across Nottingham, Derby & Mansfield with no obligation.
